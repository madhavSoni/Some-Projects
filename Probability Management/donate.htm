The discipline of probability management addresses the Flaw of Averages by radically re-thinking uncertainty in the light of current computer technology. The three goals of our open SIPmath™ Standard are to improve:

  • Communication of Uncertainties
  • Calculation with Uncertainties
  • Credibility of Uncertain Estimates
